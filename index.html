About

Recompound Strategy is a private investment firm focused on acquiring income producing assets, mainly Real Estate. All profits are reinvested to expand ownership and scale long term cash flow through continuous acquisitions and strategic allocation into other high quality assets.

What We Do

  • Real Estate: Acquire and hold cash flowing properties that fund future acquisitions.
  • Leverage: Use debt strategically to scale faster, build equity, and repay it through cash flow.
  • Reinvestment: Reinvest profits to expand ownership and accelerate compounding.
  • Diversification: Allocate into other income producing assets such as stocks, bonds, and ETFs to strengthen the portfolio.

Acquisition Blueprint

Source Target cash-flowing assets that fit strict criteria.
Acquire & Leverage Acquire with disciplined leverage, backed by cash flow.
Operate & Reinvest Improve operations and recycle free cash into more assets.
Compound Let scale, yield, and time do the heavy lifting.
“The first rule of compounding: Never interrupt it unnecessarily.” — Charlie Munger

Principles

  • Cash Flow First: If it doesn’t generate cash flow, it’s not a priority.
  • Reinvest Relentlessly: Every euro earned gets redeployed to scale.
  • Simple Structures: Keep things clean, legal, and easy to control.
  • Long-Term Horizon: Think decades ahead, not months.
  • Quiet Moves: Wealth grows in silence, not noise.
  • Control Over Everything: Own it, structure it, protect it.
  • No Dead Capital: Idle money is wasted opportunity.

Structure & Strategy

  • HoldCo Structure: All assets owned through one controlled entity for efficiency and scalability.
  • Cash Flow Engine: Income-producing properties fuel reinvestment cycles without outside dilution.
  • Capital Discipline: Debt only when self-liquidating through asset cash flow.
  • Recompound Strategy: Profits never exit — they expand ownership and equity control.
